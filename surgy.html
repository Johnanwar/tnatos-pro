Spinal Cord Injections

Back injections are considered to be among the simple treatments available. Injecting a small amount of a narcotic along with advanced compounds of Cortisone in the rear space of the vertebra makes the pain radiating from that area lighter and may eliminate it completely. This remedy is considered suitable for the following cases:

  • - Herniated Intervertebral Disc
  • - Narrow neural channel
  • - Failure of the surgical operationc
The treatment can be administered through multiple injections or just one according to the case and the amount of neural damage.

Medical cement injections

Carefully calculated quantities of medical cement with special viscosity and texture are injected with extreme precision into the body of the vertebra through using an endoscope and a radio interference device. The treatment is used to stabilise the body of the vertebra as well as prevent pain. Among the advantages of this technique are:

  • - Precise surveillance trough the radio interference device
  • - The patient remains conscious
  • - Only a local anaesthetic is needed and for a few minutes
  • - The patient is allowed to go home on the same day of the procedure
  • - Completely safe
  • - Does not result in any side effects or rise in temperature
  • - The material quickly adapts to and interacts with the body’s tissues
  • - The material lasts with the patient for his/her entire life without any complications
  • - An injection can be administered again if another vertebra gets broken
  • - Up to five injured vertebrae can be injected in one day with total safety
